TECHSHUTTLE|HACKCLUB

We are pleased to announce that our club TechShuttle has been approved with the full support of the College authorities and the global community. We are all for technology and we will strive to provide opportunities for all. We are here to create a family that is by the students, to the students and for the students and all the while we will work hard in fostering innovation and cultivating the sciences. To mark our beginning,
we are organizing a TECH WEEK and welcoming volunteer opportunities
